HTTP Status 400: Bad Request

Whoops! Let’s Fix It

This error occurs when the browser request cannot be processed due to invalid syntax or corrupted cookies. Follow the instructions below to clear brown.edu cookies and resolve the issue.

Google Chrome

Steps for clearing cookies from brown.edu in Google Chrome to resolve the browser error 400 Bad Request.

  1. Enter chrome://settings/content/all in the browser address bar
  2. Search for brown.edu
  3. Click the trash can icon to delete all of the cookies and site data for that site.
  4. Click Delete to confirm cookie removal
Learn to Clear Google Chrome Cookies

Safari (Mac)

Steps for clearing cookies from brown.edu in Safari to resolve the browser error 400 Bad Request.

  1. Click Safari from the menu
  2. Click Settings
  3. Select the Privacy tab and click Manage Website Data...
  4. Search for brown.edu and select brown.edu from the list
  5. Click Remove
  6. Click Done
Learn to Clear Safari Cookies

Firefox

Steps for clearing cookies from brown.edu in Firefox to resolve the browser error 400 Bad Request.

  1. Enter about:preferences#privacy in the browser address bar
  2. Scroll down and find the Cookies and Site Data section and click the Manage Data button
  3. Search for brown.edu and select brown.edu from the list
  4. Click Remove Selected
  5. Click Save Changes
  6. Click Remove to confirm cookie removal
Learn to Clear Firefox Cookies
