Whoops! Let’s Fix It

This error occurs when the browser request cannot be processed due to invalid syntax or corrupted cookies. You will need to clear brown.edu cookies and resolve the issue.

Learn to Refresh Your Browser by Clearing Cache and Cookies

Browsers sometimes display outdated content because they are using stored data. Clearing your cache and cookies removes these old files and ensures that pages load the most current version. Follow the step-by-step instructions below for both desktop and mobile browsers to quickly refresh your browsing experience.

Need More Help? Contact the IT Service Center.

If clearing your cookies does not resolve the issue, close your browser completely and reopen it. If the problem persists, contact the IT Service Center for assistance.
